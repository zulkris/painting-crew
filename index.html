None at all. The program is built for people starting from scratch. Day 1 covers all the fundamentals, and by Day 2 you're in a live job simulation with hands-on feedback.

Everything: the 3-day program, your equipment starter kit, full technology setup (quoting, scheduling, invoicing, client booking page), a 90-day growth roadmap, and lifetime access to the support network. There are no hidden fees or ongoing platform subscriptions.

You leave day three with equipment in hand, your technology configured, and a 90-day plan. Most graduates send their first quote within days. The speed of your first job depends on how actively you pursue clients, but you'll have everything you need from day four.
